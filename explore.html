Guided Valley Crossing

Our signature experience: a full-day guided crossing from Iten on the escarpment top, down Biretwo Road, across Chebloch Bridge, and back up the eastern Tugen Hills.

  • Depart Iten at 6:30 am — sunrise over the escarpment
  • Descent by 4WD or mountain bike via Biretwo
  • Riverbank walk at Chebloch (crocs, hornbills)
  • Traditional lunch in a valley village
  • Return via the Tugen escarpment viewpoint
