Getting Here

By road from Nairobi: Take the A104 to Eldoret, then the C51 to Iten — approx. 4.5 hours (310 km).

Public transport: Matatus run Eldoret → Iten regularly. For the valley floor, take a Tambach-bound matatu from Iten.

Nearest airport: Eldoret International Airport (ELD), ~65 km from Iten.

Best Time to Visit

Jan – Feb / Jun – Sep: Dry season — ideal for hiking, cycling, and photography. Clear skies, cool escarpment mornings.

Mar – May / Oct – Dec: Long and short rains. The valley turns green, waterfalls appear, and birdlife peaks — but some roads get muddy.

What to Pack
  • Layers — escarpment mornings are cold (12–16 °C); valley floor hits 30 °C+
  • Sun protection and water (valley floor sun is intense)
  • Hiking shoes for gorge trails
  • Binoculars for birds and valley views
  • Camera with a wide lens for escarpment panoramas
  • Cash (KES) — ATMs only in Iten and Tambach
